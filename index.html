About Me

I am a labor economist and an Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics and the Jeb E. Brooks School of Public Policy at Cornell University. I study inequality with a particular focus on criminal justice and education.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from Harvard University in 2024. I hold a B.A. in Mathematics and Economics from Wesleyan University. Here is my CV.
